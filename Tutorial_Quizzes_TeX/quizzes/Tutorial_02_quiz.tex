\textbf{Time:} 10 minutes\\
\textbf{Format:} pen \& paper

\subsection*{Q1 (PDF/CDF + transformation)}
Let $X$ have pdf $f_X(x)=2x$ for $x\in[0,1]$. Define $Y=X^2$.

\begin{enumerate}
\item Find the CDF of $Y$.
\item Find the pdf of $Y$.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Q2 (central limit intuition)}
Suppose $X_i$ are i.i.d. with mean $\mu$ and variance $\sigma^2$. Explain (in 1--2 sentences) what happens to the distribution of $\bar X=\frac{1}{n}\sum_i X_i$ as $n$ grows, and give its approximate mean and variance.
